Ecosystems & Energy Flow

Detailed module on trophic levels, biomass transfer, and nutrient cycling.

menu_book Section 1: Introduction to Trophic Levels

Students will investigate how energy enters ecosystems through primary productivity and moves through various trophic levels. This section covers the fundamental difference between autotrophs and heterotrophs.

Key Concept

The 10% Rule: Only about 10% of the energy at one trophic level is passed on to the next.

Required Media

Energy Pyramid Diagram, Video: Yellowstone Food Web Dynamics.

science Section 2: Nutrient Cycling & Decomposition

While energy flows in one direction, matter cycles. This lesson focuses on the Carbon and Nitrogen cycles and the critical role of decomposers in returning nutrients to the soil.
